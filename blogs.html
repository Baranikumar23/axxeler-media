How We Generate E-Commerce Leads at an Incredible CPC of Just ₹2

PUBLISHED: JUNE 1, 2026 ● AUTHOR: AXXELER ADS TEAM ● 8 MIN READ

In the highly competitive world of e-commerce, customer acquisition cost (CAC) is the ultimate metric that determines whether a brand scales to millions in revenue or runs out of cash. Most brands today are struggling with skyrocketing CPMs, generic creatives, and mediocre CTRs, leading to average Cost-Per-Clicks (CPC) ranging from ₹15 to ₹40 on Meta channels.

At Axxeler, we broke standard paradigms. For our major client, Gameon Solution (gameonsolution.in), we deployed a proprietary creative testing and structural bidding system that generated high-intent purchase leads at a record-shattering ₹2 Cost-Per-Click (CPC).

"If you rely purely on standard media buying configurations, you inherit market average costs. Low-cost customer acquisition is won through extreme creative relevance and script engineering."

The Core Strategy: Scroll-Stopping Short-Form Videos

The Facebook and Instagram algorithms assign a Relevance Score (now divided into Quality Ranking, Engagement Rate Ranking, and Conversion Rate Ranking) to every ad creative. The higher the relevance score, the less the ad network charges to place your ad in auction spots.

To drop CPCs to ₹2, we implemented the following creative pillars:

  • The 3-Second Hook: We tested 15 different visual hooks in the first 3 seconds of our short-form content. An aggressive hook—such as showing a product comparison or displaying a controversial statement—immediately stops user scrolling, spiking CTRs to over 5.4%.
  • High-Tempo Editorial Layouts: Utilizing quick visual transitions, high-contrast text overlays, and dynamic background rhythms, we kept user attention locked in. Average watch durations exceeded 85% of the video duration.
  • Direct-Response Copy: Our copywriting didn't focus on brand fluff. We addressed the target audience's direct pain point and provided a frictionless single call-to-action (CTA).

Key Campaign Metrics (Gameon Solution)

● Average Ad CTR: 5.82% (vs 0.9% industry standard)

● Blended CPC achieved: ₹1.94 to ₹2.12

● Overall Campaign ROAS: 12.4x

● Lead-to-Purchase Conversion: 8.4% within 14 days

Media Buying & Bidding Architecture

We avoided standard 'Detailed Targeting' configurations which are expensive due to heavy agency overlap bidding. Instead, we deployed a Broad Targeting strategy with demographic exclusions. We let the creative itself act as our targeting filter.

We leveraged Advantage+ Budget Optimization combined with cost-cap bidding thresholds set at exactly ₹3.5. This forced the system to only serve impressions during low-competition auction cycles when the absolute highest probability conversion clicks were available. Once the algorithm identified our highly relevant viral creatives, the click volume spiked, maintaining a stable ₹2 CPC even as budgets scaled.

Cracking the Code: Real Estate Lead Generation at a Record-Low ₹4 CPC

PUBLISHED: MAY 24, 2026 ● AUTHOR: AXXELER SEARCH TEAM ● 10 MIN READ

Real estate lead generation is widely regarded as one of the most expensive segments in digital advertising. With major developers and aggregate portals bidding millions on high-intent terms like "buy apartment", "villa for sale", and "premium flats", cost-per-click values regularly spike above ₹80, resulting in average Cost-Per-Lead (CPL) prices in the ₹800 to ₹2,500 range.

At Axxeler, we refused to accept these exorbitant costs as 'unavoidable market rates'. By integrating hyper-local search intent systems, Google My Business (GMB) mapping overlays, and highly optimized geo-fenced Meta campaigns, we developed a system that generates highly qualified real estate leads at an unbelievable ₹4 Cost-Per-Click (CPC).

"When everyone else is bidding on high-volume national search terms, the secret to massive margins lies in local intent capture, map position dominance, and localized landing page speed."

The Multi-Channel Funnel: GMB + Hyper-Local Ads

We discovered that buyers looking to acquire apartments or premium real estate search using highly regional criteria. Instead of targeting generic terms across broad metropolitan areas, we deployed a dual-channel localized funnel:

  • GMB Dominance (Local Maps): We optimized our client GMB listings by introducing geo-tagged image clusters, structured localized service schemas, and automated customer response triggers. This ranked their properties in the coveted top 3 map results organically.
  • Geo-Fenced Micro-Campaigns: We launched Google Search and Meta Ads geo-fenced strictly within a 3-mile radius of high-net-worth business hubs and tech parks. The ad creatives featured specific local landmarks, driving high relevance and CTRs.
  • Ultra-Fast Local Landing Pages: To maintain high conversion rates, we engineered lightweight landing pages that loaded in under 400ms. Frictionless single-tap auto-fill forms captured prospect details in seconds.

Key Campaign Results (Real Estate Portal)

● Target Geographic Radius: 3-mile business hubs

● Cost Per Click (CPC) Secured: ₹3.80 - ₹4.20

● Lead Conversion Rate: 11.2% of page visits

● Average Cost-Per-Lead (CPL): ₹35.70 (market average: ₹1,200)

Why It Works: The Cost Dynamics

By keeping location boundaries extremely tight and targeting micro-neighborhoods, we avoided competing directly in national bidding auctions. Additionally, our highly specific ad copy (e.g. "Move into your new flat in Sector 62 this week") filtered out window-shoppers instantly, maintaining high relevancy scores on ad networks and dropping CPC to ₹4.

Because these prospects are hyper-local, their purchase intent is significantly higher than cold leads, resulting in an extraordinary closing ratio for our clients' sales agents.

Speed Equals Revenue: The 100ms Website CRO Impact

PUBLISHED: MAY 15, 2026 ● AUTHOR: AXXELER DEV TEAM ● 6 MIN READ

Most marketers allocate their entire budget toward ad creatives, target variables, and bidding caps, yet completely ignore the single most critical touchpoint in the customer journey: the landing page itself. If your advertising redirects high-intent users to a sluggish, bloated site, you are literally throwing cash away.

Data from major technology providers confirms a brutal reality: a mere 100ms delay in website loading speed can decrease overall conversion rates by up to 7%. Let's look at the technical code parameters showing why speed is the ultimate conversion multiplier, and how premium semantic engineering can boost your advertising return instantly.

Why Bloated Builders Are Killing Your Retainer ROAS

Many modern agencies build landing pages utilizing heavy drag-and-drop CMS builders (such as WordPress Elementor, Wix, or custom bloated themes). These tools inject thousands of unnecessary CSS classes, duplicate jQuery scripts, and massive unoptimized graphic assets. This results in heavy page weight, causing mobile loading times to exceed 4 to 8 seconds on standard cellular connections.

When a user clicks your Meta or Google ad, they expect instant gratification. If the page doesn't load within 2 seconds, over 53% of mobile users will bounce. This means you are paying ad networks for clicks that never even render your tracking pixels—skyrocketing your real Cost-Per-Lead.

The Solution: Clean Semantic Engineering

At Axxeler, we build website interfaces using senior-level engineering practices:

  • Semantic HTML5 Structure: No unnecessary container nestings. We write ultra-clean HTML to maximize parsing speeds on browsers.
  • Vanilla CSS Variable Layouts: We style using curated custom variables, modern grids, and sleek, performant transitions that run directly on GPUs without heavy scripts.
  • Vanilla JavaScript (ES6+): We replace massive library suites (like jQuery) with responsive vanilla JS codes. Our scripts execute asynchronously, preventing rendering blockages.
