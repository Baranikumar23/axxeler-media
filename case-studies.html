Nook Pickleball

Nook Pickleball operates a premium regional pickleball venue. While the physical facilities are state-of-the-art, their local digital presence was weak, resulting in low organic search rankings, half-empty court listings, and sluggish membership acquisitions.

Axxeler engineered a specialized Local Search Domination Strategy. We restructured their Google My Business listing using geotagged graphics and automated customer maps interaction pathways. We coupled GMB maps optimization with micro-location Meta ads to secure local court bookings.

The Local SEO Playbook

1. Local GMB SEO

Injected targeted regional keywords (e.g. "pickleball court near me", "indoor court") and automated map trigger responses, claiming the organic top spot.

2. 3-Mile Geo Ads

Served highly targeted local ads containing regional references to mobile users in business tech parks located within 3 miles of the facility.

3. High-Converting Booking CRO

Simplified their online reservation funnel from 8 steps to a frictionless 2-step click system, boosting booking conversion rates immediately.
