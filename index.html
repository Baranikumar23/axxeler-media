Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about partnering with Axxeler Media.

How can you generate CPCs as low as ₹2 for E-commerce? +

We leverage advanced creative testing frameworks. By deploying high-tempo short-form creatives and narrative hook copywriting, the Facebook/Instagram ad algorithm scores our ads with high relevance, drastically lowering ad delivery costs and generating ultra-qualified clicks at ₹2 CPC.

Is ₹4 CPC really possible for competitive Real Estate? +

Yes. Traditional agencies launch broad location targeting, which triggers massive bidding wars. We use hyper-local geo-fencing, combined with negative keyword list scrubbing and local GMB map triggers, to capture low-competition, high-intent prospective buyers at a fraction of standard ad network rates.

Do I need to manage or shoot video content myself? +

No. We provide comprehensive Content Marketing execution. Our specialized strategists outline viral scripts, coordinate filming parameters, handle editing, add high-engagement dynamic captions, and schedule delivery across social assets.

How long before we see compounding SEO results? +

Paid ad systems start delivering sales metrics within 7 to 14 days. For organic search engine rankings, indexing changes, and deep SEO clusters, we suggest a 3 to 6-month campaign scope to establish stable organic domination.
